## Pros And Cons

| Role | Pros | Cons |
|---|---|---|
| Agricultural GIS Analyst | Perfect bridge between GIS, agriculture, Uruguay, and income | Must prove with real case studies; weak if only maps |
| Soil Mapping Specialist | Directly supports Ciencias del Suelo | Harder technically; requires careful scientific language |
| Crop Monitoring Specialist | Clear freelance demand, NDVI is portfolio-friendly | Many competitors; must avoid overclaiming agronomic conclusions |
| Land Suitability Analyst | Easy to package as a paid service | Requires transparent assumptions and limitations |
| Web GIS Developer | Better income ceiling | Distracts from soil science if started too early |
