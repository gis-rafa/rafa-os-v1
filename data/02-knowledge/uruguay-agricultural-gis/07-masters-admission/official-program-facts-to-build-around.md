## Official Program Facts To Build Around

UdelaR/Fagro's Maestria en Ciencias Agrarias is a 2-year research-oriented master's. The CAP page states students complete 100 credits: 60 in courses/activities and 40 in thesis work. The final work is scientific research. Applicants need a university degree and documents including inscription form, objective/metas letter, transcript, degree title, and thesis director/coordinator approvals. Foreign students need passport copy, two recommendation letters from professionals in their country, and legalized degree.
